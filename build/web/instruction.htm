Monthly Progress Report of MSME-DIs
Steps Involved in Online Data Entry of Monthly Progress Report

     Procedure :

       STEP 1.)  First, Enter your respective ID and password which has been already assigned to indiviual MSME-DIs

       STEP 2.) After login click in left side of window and then click on Entry form of MPR Then click on Enter your Target of Monthly Progress Report to set target of various programmes in different forms ,The DIs can also edit if the target of programmes are revised

       STEP 3.) After that click on the Training Programmes here MSME-Dis Can only enter Monthly Achevment during the month, No need to Enter Monthly Achievment up to the Months (CUMULATIVE Data)

     

       

       STEP 4.) To fill Iso Detail, click on the link Status of Iso here also you have to enter data int the Data of the Month Columns Only,no need to fill cumulative

     

       

       STEP 5.) To fill VDP Detail, click on the link Entry form of VDP here also you have to enter data int the Monthly Data Columns Only,no need to fill cumulative Columns

     

       

       STEP 6.) To fill worksop Detail, click on the link Workshop Details here you have to enter data int the Monthly Achievment Columns Only,no need to fill cumulative Columns

     

       

       STEP 7.) To fill Report Form Detail, click on the link Report form here you have to enter Monthly Achievment Columns Only,no need to fill cumulative Columns and Target columns these are automatically feeded columns

     

       

    STEP 8.)  Same procedure to be followed to enter data in All Monthly progress Report forms ,point to be noted is that in the Entry forms Target and Cumulative columns are readonly fields or they are calculated fields, no need to enter data in that fields

       STEP 9.)  Revenue Earning form are divided in two parts Revenue Earning and Revenue Earning Barnches ,Revenue Earning Barnches is for Msme-DIs having branch offices , they can fill both the forms

     

       STEP 11.) Field shouldn't be left blank either you insert data of the Month or insert zero in the blank text boxes
                     

       STEP 12.) You can also edit data once after submitting the forms

       STEP 13.)  After filling all the forms or single form, click on theReport Generation to generate the report ,MSME-DIs can generate Report of single form as well as as all the forms ,Consolidated Report will be generated after filling all the form click on the Monthly Progress ReportLlink to generate over all Reports .

  STEP 14.) click on respective name of the form to generate report of single form
